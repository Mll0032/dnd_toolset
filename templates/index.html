Invar

Deep within the ruins of an ancient dungeon, a being of arcane brilliance slumbers—Invar, the last of the Elven Item Historians. Once mortal, Invar's insatiable thirst for knowledge led him to the forbidden halls of the Evervault, a sanctum of magical artifacts and forbidden relics. Betrayed by his own insatiable fascination for items. He was shackled to the vault by the dungeons insane inhabitants as its eternal guardian, Invar was transformed into a magical construct, bound to document every item of power that exists across the realms.

The Ones Who Freed Invar

For centuries, Invar remained imprisoned, his form pulsing with a dim, violet glow—a mere whisper of the scholar he once was. That was until a band of adventurers—a warrior monk, a twilight cleric, a cunning thief, and a warlock bound by eldritch oaths— discovered the dungeon and broke the arcane chains that held him captive. Now free, Invar continues his life's work, chronicling the magical artifacts of the world so that none may be forgotten.

Discover the Arcane

Now, you have access to Invar’s ever-growing collection. Whether you seek legendary weapons, enchanted armor, or the rarest magical trinkets, his archives are open to all who dare to explore them. Delve into the depths, filter by source, and uncover the secrets of forgotten relics.
